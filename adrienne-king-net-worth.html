FAQs about Adrienne King

Who is Adrienne King?

She is an American actress and visual artist best known for her role as Alice Hardy in the original Friday the 13th films.

How old is Adrienne King?

As of 2024, she is 68 years old. She was born on July 21, 1955, in Oyster Bay, New York .

What are some notable films Adrienne King has appeared in?

Besides Friday the 13th (1980) and Friday the 13th Part 2 (1981), King has appeared in Saturday Night Fever (1977), The Butterfly Room (2012), and Jason Rising (2021).

Why did Adrienne leave acting after Friday the 13th Part 2?

She left acting primarily due to a traumatic experience with a stalker after the release of the first Friday the 13th film.

What is Adrienne King’s involvement in the wine industry?

She is involved in the wine industry through her own line of Friday the 13th-themed wines called Crystal Lake Wines. She runs this venture from her home in Oregon.

Has Adrienne returned to acting recently?

Yes, King has returned to acting in recent years, appearing in films such as Psychic Experiment (2009) and Killer Therapy (2019). She also reprised her role as Alice Hardy in the fan film Jason Rising (2021) .

What is Adrienne’s educational background?

She attended the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) in London after her initial success in acting and following her stalker ordeal .

Is Adrienne King married?

Yes, she is married to Richard Hassanein. The couple has been together for nearly 29 years.

What notable voice roles has she performed?

King has provided voice looping for many major films, including Philadelphia, The Pelican Brief, Titanic, and Almost Famous. She also narrated the audiobook for Grady Hendrix’s novel The Final Girl Support Group .
