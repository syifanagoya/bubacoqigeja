Before meeting Prince Harry, Meghan Markle dreamed of taking the place of Gwyneth Paltrow

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Now it’s quite difficult to imagine what Meghan Markle‘s life would have been like if she hadn’t met Prince Harry because the actress had a tremendous impact on his life. The answer to this ambiguous question was tried to be given by the journalist Tina Brown, who has been widely heard lately due to the release of the book Palace Documents. The author’s work is filled with the most controversial facts from the biography of the royal family; it is full of sad revelations about how Prince Philip felt at Lady Di’s funeral and how Kate Middleton was relaxing with her friend’s karaoke at a bachelorette party. 

In the chapters about Meghan Markle’s pre-royal life, readers will also learn about her ambitions. Even during the filming of the acclaimed series “Force Majeure,” with the participation of the future duchess, Megan devoted all her energy to her blog called The Tig. At that time, the actress was sure that she had a chance to repeat the success of Goop, founded by Gwyneth Paltrow. So, as everyone knows, the actress started with interesting notes or personal observations on the topic of beauty and health. After that, she created a commercial site with her own line of natural cosmetics, household products, etc. 

In April 2017, Meghan closed her favorite blog about life because much more serious changes awaited her ahead.

“After nearly three great years of this adventure with you, it’s time to say goodbye to The Tig. But, above all, never forget your worth. Thank you for everything,” Markle wrote.
