FAQs About Lauren Bacall

How did she start her acting career?

She was discovered by director Howard Hawks when she appeared on the cover of Harper’s Bazaar at age 19. Hawks cast her in her debut film, To Have and Have Not.

What was her real name?

She was born Betty Joan Perske.

Did she receive any major awards during her career?

Yes, she won two Tony Awards and received an honorary Academy Award in 2009 for her contributions to the film industry.

Who was she married to?

She was famously married to Humphrey Bogart and later had a relationship with Frank Sinatra.

What is she known for in the film industry?

She is known for her distinctive husky voice and performances in film noir during Hollywood’s Golden Age, as well as her iconic roles alongside Humphrey Bogart.

What were some of her notable films?

Noteworthy films include The Big Sleep, Key Largo, and To Have and Have Not.

Did she write any books?

Yes, she wrote two autobiographies: Lauren Bacall By Myself and By Myself and Then Some, the former of which won a National Book Award.

What other interests did she have outside of acting?

She had a deep affection for dogs, particularly her Papillon, Sophie, who was often seen with her at social events.

How did she contribute to the theatre?

Besides her film career, she was also a celebrated stage actress, appearing in Broadway hits like Applause and Woman of the Year, for which she received her Tony Awards.

What legacy did she leave behind?

She is remembered as a film and fashion icon, with a career that spanned over seven decades, influencing many in and outside of Hollywood.
