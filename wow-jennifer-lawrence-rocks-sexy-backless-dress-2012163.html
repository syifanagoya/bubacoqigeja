Wow! Jennifer Lawrence Rocks Sexy Backless Dress

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Out with the gold.

After wearing two dazzling — yet similar — gilded gowns at the Los Angeles and London premieres of The Hunger Games, Jennifer Lawrence switched it up for the film’s Paris debut on Thursday.

The 21-year-old leading lady rocked a sleek black Tom Ford dress with zipper detail that snaked around the open-back design to her hip.

She accessorized with Tom Ford gold platform heels, a bold red lip and a sleek hairstyle. This is the first time the actress wore her hair down and straight. Her previous ‘dos include an embellished braided updo and a loose ponytail.
