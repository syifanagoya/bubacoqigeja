Name:Caroline Manzo

Hometown:Brooklyn, NY

Birthday:August 23, 1961

About

Caroline Manzo is a cast member on the reality series The Real Housewives of New Jersey. Like her fellow cast members, she lives in the town of Franklin Lakes, N.J. She is married to Albert Manzo who co-owns the event and catering facility The Brownstone with his brother, Tommy, who is real housewife Dina Manzo‘s husband. Caroline Manzo has three children: Albert Jr., Lauren and Christopher. Manzo owns Opus Properties, a real estate firm and a children’s accessories line. She also serves on the board of Dina’s non-profit foundation, Project Ladybug.

Manzo is known on The Real Housewives for being very protective of her family. Her famous line from the show about her family is, “We are as thick as thieves and we will protect each other until the end.”
