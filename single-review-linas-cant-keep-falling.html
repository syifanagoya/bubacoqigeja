SINGLE REVIEW: Lina's 'Can't Keep Falling'

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Lina made history back in 2013 by becoming the very first American-Palestinian to win The Voice Israel. Since her stunning victory she has gained a massive fanbase in Israel and is gaining supporters across the globe every day. Even superstars Alicia Keys and Adam Lambert are fans of Lina’s sultry vocals.

Her new single ‘Can’t Keep Falling’ is the first track to be lifted off her upcoming debut album which will be released later this year. The song is about a rocky relationship, one that you know you should quit but can’t quite seem to walk away.

The Voice Israel winner’s vocals have shades of Selena Gomez’s and the tune is similar to something Ariana Grande would release. It is a very pure pop track and Lina’s vocals are super sexy and sultry and the bouncy beat and catchy chorus is very radio friendly.

It seems there is only a matter of time before Lina becomes a massive pop star across the globe and she is definitely one to watch this year. ‘Can’t Keep Falling’ is a really great pop song that will get stuck on a loop in your head for days.

Fans of Selena Gomez, Ariana Grande, and Demi Lovato, and Cheryl will no doubt feel a connection to the rising star.

RATING: 4.5/5.
