Rashawnna Guy (born January 3, 1978) is an American rapper who goes by the stage name Shawnna. Ludacris’ Disturbing tha Peace Records signed her as the first female artist to Def Jam South. She is the daughter of blues artist Buddy Guy and a former member of the female Chicago rap group Infamous Syndicate. Her fast-paced delivery, as well as her sexually graphic appearance and lyrics, have made her famous. With “Stand Up” with Ludacris in 2003, she is one of just five female rappers (the others being Lauryn Hill, Lil’ Kim, Remy Ma, and Iggy Azalea) to ever have a number-one single on the US Billboard Hot 100.
